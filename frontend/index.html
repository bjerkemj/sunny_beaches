Welcome to Sunny Beaches

This webpage shows the conditions at three beaches in proximity of Brisbane. The colored square gives an indication of the surf based on weathertype and swell. There are four different colors, ranging from perfect to bad. These colors in the same order are: limegreen, mossgreen, yellow and red. The beaches permanently shown are mostly in the mossgreen and yellow category. If you wish to see a place with red conditions check out Antalya in Turkey, with coordinates: 36.90812 30.69556
